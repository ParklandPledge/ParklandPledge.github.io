THE PLEDGE
1. I pledge to speak up for gun safety legislation.
2. I pledge to not accept campaign donations from the NRA or to donate to NRA-supported candidates.
3. I pledge to support scientific research into the epidemic of gun violence, and to work to prevent the sale of assault weapons and their deadly accessories.
4. I pledge to put policy above party for nonpartisan gun reform.
5. I pledge to speak truth to power and hold elected officials accountable for their decisions.
ABOUT US
The Parkland Pledge is a student-lead organization based in central New Jersey that aims to end gun violence by educating students and giving them a platform to express their wishes for common-sense gun reform.
